From humble beginnings, 3 Men and a Bistro has risen to formidable status in the local Foodie scene.
Founded in 1997, the fledgling company took over a very small enhanced coffee stand and began introducing their new fusion flavorings to their stock menu and have been building their menu from there ever since.
The company has since grown in popularity and reputation.
